About me
Hi, my name is Rosita Keonti. I have spent the last 7 years finding my inner peace through various types of meditation, spiritualism and philosophy. Last 3 years I have spent travelling around Asia meeting countless teachers, students and masters who also try to find peace and way of their spirit.

Don't think that I'm some kind of shaman or I believe in magic, those things that I've been practising and acquiring through past years were meant to help me find myself in today's world. I don't encourage you to spend the rest of your life meditating naked on the stone in high mountains without food or water (but it needs to be said that this experience is one of it's kind).

I found my way (or should I say, the world found the way for me) and I want to help you with finding yours, without travelling around the world and dozens of hours of waiting for the master to finish his meditation. Instead i want to guide you through your own spiritual way, at your pace, peacefully in your home. The only thing you need is to have some spare time for yourself and willingness to try.
